Birthday

in a box!

Our hope is that every child, despite their living conditions, can feel celebrated on their birthday!

When you sponsor a Birthday in a Box, we will deliver it to foster care communities and child protective services so that kids can still experience the power of a celebration!

Each box provides case managers with all the supplies necessary to throw a rockin' party for their kiddos! This box is meant to be a fun keepsake for the birthday child!

Thanks to our friends Gifts for the Good Life for designing these boxes!
